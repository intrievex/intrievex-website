About Us

History

Intrievex was started in 2010 with the mission of becoming a premier information retrieval technology and services company.

 

Our goal is to make the science of document and information retrieval simple, reliable, and valuable for your business. In January 2011, Intrievex acquired CourtsOnline to serve as its flagship product to provide information retrieval services for court case records.

 

Intrievex is very excited about providing additional resources and offerings to build upon the foundation that makes CourtsOnline successful.

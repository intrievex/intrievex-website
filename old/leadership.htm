About Us

Leadership

Rick Myers

President

Rick was raised in Seattle and began his professional career upon graduation from the University of Washington with a degree in finance. Rick has over 20 years of operational and technical business experience from working in and consulting with small family-owned businesses, technology startups, and Fortune 500 companies. He was director of IT during his tenure at McCaw Cellular/AT&T Wireless Services, leading a team of 100 people and a $20 million budget in customer acquisition and risk management. Rick then worked with the $500 million startup Terabeam, where he was a director of operations for three years deploying their technology to new markets. After leaving Terabeam, Rick purchased Sinometrics, a translation company, which he tripled in size and sold in 2008. He has a passion for small business, growing companies, and leading people.

Curt Whitton

Vice President

Curt was raised in Seattle and began his professional career upon graduation from Linfield College in McMinnville, OR with a degree in finance. In total, he has over 20 years of business, technical, sales, and project management experience working at small consulting companies, IBM, and Codesic Consulting. At Codesic, Curt was co-owner and COO. Under Curt's leadership, Codesic doubled its consulting practice to over $21 million until it was acquired by CGI Group Inc. in 2007. His primary responsibilities included large account management, HR, strategy, IT management, and personnel development. Prior to starting Intrievex, Curt launched Trinity Capital with Rick Myers and Ed Pepin, investing in small companies, properties, and consulting small businesses. He is actively involved in his church, coaching his children's sports teams, and enjoying the outdoors.

Chris Masterson

Director of Development

Chris' background includes over 25 years experience as a hardware and software engineer. He has developed or managed a number of commercially successful products for companies such as Bristol-Meyers Squibb, Unisys, AT&T, Microsoft Corporation, Spacelabs Medical, and Memorex. Chris received his diploma in aviation science while a member of the U.S. Air Force in 1978. Chris enjoys a passion for cooking and writing music.

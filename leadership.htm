Leadership

Rick Myers

President

Rick is the President and Owner of Intrievex, Inc. which he co-founded in 2010. He previously purchased and operated Sinometrics, a translation company, which he tripled in size over 5 years and sold in 2008.

 

Rick has over 20 years of operational and technical business experience from working in and consulting with small family-owned businesses, technology startups, and Fortune 500 companies including 10 years in the Wireless industry in management roles.

 

Rick also spends time in his Management Consultant business, advising businesses and doing Mergers and Acquisitions. He has a passion for small business, growing companies, and leading people. He was born and has lived the majority of his life in Seattle and has a finance degree from the University of Washington.

 

Curt Whitton

Vice President

Curt was raised in Seattle and began his professional career upon graduation from Linfield College in McMinnville, OR with a degree in finance. In total, he has over 20 years of business, technical, sales, and project management experience working at small consulting companies, IBM, and Codesic Consulting.

 

At Codesic, Curt was co-owner and COO. Under Curt's leadership, Codesic doubled its consulting practice to over $21 million until it was acquired by CGI Group Inc. in 2007. His primary responsibilities included large account management, HR, strategy, IT management, and personnel development.

 

Prior to starting Intrievex, Curt launched Trinity Capital with Rick Myers and Ed Pepin, investing in small companies, properties, and consulting small businesses. He is actively involved in his church, coaching his children's sports teams, and enjoying the outdoors.

 

Chris Masterson

Director of Development

 

Chris' background includes over 25 years experience as a hardware and software engineer.

 

He has developed or managed a number of commercially successful products for companies such as Bristol-Meyers Squibb, Unisys, AT&T, Microsoft Corporation, Spacelabs Medical, and Memorex. Chris received his diploma in aviation science while a member of the U.S. Air Force in 1978. Chris enjoys a passion for cooking and writing music.

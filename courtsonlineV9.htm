CourtsOnline v9.0

Our flagship application, CourtsOnline (COL), uses its proprietary technology to provide online access, searching, and retrieval of court case records. We provide online searching for all cases associated with a specific person or business in one or more courts. In addition, we provide searching for a particular case number in a selected court. For online access clients, Intrievex distributes a user interface application via its website. This application makes searching for a court case a very easy and efficient process in what would otherwise be a slow, difficult, and tedious experience when done in person at the courthouse. Furthermore, access is available 24 hours a day, 365 days a week; making court records research possible when needed, rather than just during the court's open hours.

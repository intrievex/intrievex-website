Guiding Principles

We believe there's a better way to find information. We believe that a search experience should be easy and simple. And we believe it needs to be faster and more inexpensive. It is an exciting time for us at Intrievex and for our loyal customers.

 

As we have aggressively transitioned our little company into a more flexibile and modern enterprise, our products, services and support have matured as well. But we think is just a start. We are young and energetic and we think we are on to something with our vision and our approach.

 

We have seen a need for disruption in the industries we serve and as an example of that, now with our flagship product CourtsOnline, our customers finally have a better way to find information. We think you will enjoy your search experience much more now, and we look forward to partnering with you to continually improved it. Good things have happened.

 

Greater things are still to come and we appreciate you being a part of it.

 

Thanks for choosing Intrievex.
